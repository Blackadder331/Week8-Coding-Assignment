About us

We have worked on a variety of projects and have deep experience in residential work. Our practice draws inspiration from the unique culture and climate of this region. We look for the special qualities of each particular place, including the history of its architecture and landscape. We carefully consider each building site for views, privacy, sun angles, and tree cover. We make sure that we take advantage of opportunities for natural light, passive heating, and ventilation. It is important to understand the neighborhood and landscape so that each house improves its surroundings for the benefit of your family and the community.




Robert Sharp
Architect

Robert Sharp has 25 years of experience in the architectural profession. He founded Robert Sharp, Architect in 1998 to specialize in serving the needs of real estate developers, non profits, and communities. Robert typically work in New Urban, Traditional Neighborhood, and Infill settings. Robert has a keen interest in city and town planning, development economics, and regional architecture.
